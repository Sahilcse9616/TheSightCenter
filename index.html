Why Us?

The Sight center Clinic was established in 2017 by D.r. Pooja Sachan (Optom) who is the only Graduate Optometrist from world famous “NIMS University- Jaipur”. D.r. Pooja specializes into Vision Therapy, Low Vision Aids, RGP & Keratoconus contact lens fittings and other non-surgical modes of vision correction and enhancement. The Sight center Clinic is equipped with state of art Zero Error Digital Eye Exam technology which ensures maximum precision in measuring refractive errors of the eye (Eye Power).

From Routine Exams to advanced Cataract, LASIK ,Contact lens Clinic and other Minor & major Eye disease procedures to an extensive optical selection, we’ll meet all your eye care needs in one place.

D.r. Pooja sachan has received many accolades for her work in the field of eye care. Her company donates about 1500 Eye Glasses for free to the government school children and economically backward section of the Society.
